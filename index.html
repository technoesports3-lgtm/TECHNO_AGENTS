Inside the Engine

How Our AI Voice Agents Process Every Call

From the moment a lead dials your number to the instant your CRM updates, our voice pipeline works in real time — with zero human delay. Here's the exact sequence your business runs on.

1. Call Initiated

Lead dials your published number (or clicks from Google Ad). Our AI instantly picks up — no rings, no wait.

2. AI Answers & Transcribes

Speech-to-Text converts the conversation in real time. Natural language understanding extracts intent, name, and query.

3. Intent Analysis & Enrichment

Our engine scores lead quality, appends firmographic data via Clearbit, and decides next action — all within 800ms.

4. CRM Sync & Alert

Lead pushed to your CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot/Zoho). Your sales team gets an instant WhatsApp/Slack alert with a call summary.
